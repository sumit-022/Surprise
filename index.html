Our Love Story

First Meet

27 November 2021

It all started with Hey! untill it became Bae!. I personally feel that I met the destiny of my life back then. Although I didn't know that you're gonna be this much important to me.

First Date

10 October 2022

Finally, our first date! Although it wasn't the most romantic date, but it was the most meaningful and unforgettable moment in my life. We had street chicken roll for the first time at the palassio stall and then it became the most special one out of all the stalls ou there. You were the most comforting person there and all I ever wanted was to see you smile. As we stood there, savoring every bite of those delicious street chicken rolls, I couldn't help but feel a connection growing between us. The bustling street around us seemed to fade away, and it was just you and me, sharing this incredible moment. Our conversation flowed effortlessly, as if we had known each other for a lifetime. We talked about our home, our most embarrassing childhood memories and also your usual bak-bak. I was captivated by your stories and the way your eyes lit up when you spoke about your passions.

Start Relationship

- 25 July 2022

Sooo, finally, The start of a never-ending thing❤️. Who thought the start can this grand as ours. Both in formals proposing each other. And then both of them on their knees. And then the most wonderful and special kiss of my life. Okay, so technically, this should be above the first date coz we started off and then went on a date. But like yeah, who cares. You would still love me the same right? Anyways, moving on to the start. Who can forget the most beautiful ring which you gave me as a symbol of your love and commitment? It sparkled with the promise of forever, just like the twinkle in your eyes that night. I couldn't have asked for a more perfect beginning to our journey together. That first formal proposal was a moment frozen in time, a memory etched into my heart. Our love story is a unique one, filled with unexpected twists and turns, and I wouldn't change a thing about it. It's our own special narrative, and it's just the beginning of the adventure we're embarking on.

1 Year

25 July 2023

And then comes the most special moment in this relationship. We celebrated our 1 year together. All those times of being sad and being happy. Laughing together, crying together and doing everything together. We finally made it up to a year and still counting. I know we have a long way to go but I am sure we will make it to the end. I love you so much and I am so grateful to have you. I want to thank you for everything you have done for me. I know I am not the easiest person to be with but you still chose me. I love you so much and I promise to love you forever and always. PS: I am sorry for all the times I have hurt you. I promise to never do that again.
